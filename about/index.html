Leroy Gonzales

Master Pastry Chef & Founder

With over 15 years of experience in the culinary world, Leroy Gonzales has perfected the art of crafting exquisite, homemade cheesecakes. Starting his journey in his mother's kitchen, he learned the importance of using only the finest ingredients and traditional techniques.

After working in some of the most prestigious restaurants across the country, he returned home with a dream: to create the perfect cheesecakes that would bring joy to every customer's day.

Today, Leroy's passion is baked into every one of our desserts, where every cheesecake is handcrafted with love, precision, and the finest ingredients sourced from local farms and premium suppliers worldwide.

The Mr. Cheesecake Story

How It All Started

Mr. Cheesecake opened its doors in 2018 with a simple mission: to create the most delicious and beautiful cheesecakes for our community. We believe in quality above all.

What started as a small neighborhood bakery has grown into a beloved destination for cheesecake lovers from across the region, but we've never forgotten our roots or compromised on quality.

Our Promise to You

Every cheesecake that leaves our kitchen is a testament to our commitment to excellence. We guarantee that every ingredient, from start to finish, maintains the highest standards of quality, and treat every customer like family.

Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or simply treating yourself to something sweet, we're honored to be a part of your memorable moments.
